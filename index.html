About Candy & Co

Candy & Co is a bridal makeup and hair company serving New York, California, Austin, and select cities in Florida. Candace and her team have been in the beauty industry for over 15 years and are passionate about making brides look and feel elegant. We have expert knowledge of beauty products and utilize the latest tips and tricks for makeup application and hairstyles. We will bring out your most natural qualities, making you glow on your special day.
